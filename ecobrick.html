ECO BRICKING QUEST

Let's explore this quest by learning the magic of eco-bricking step by step!

LEVEL 0:
Plastic Hunter
Let's find the right type of plastic for our ecobricking quest !
LEVEL 1:
The Cleaner
Let's scrub off all dirt, oil, and food. Dry those plastics – no moisture allowed!
LEVEL 2:
Solid Base Builder
Let's now place soft plastics at the bottom to create a solid base!
LEVEL 3:
Cut n Stuff
Now, cut plastic into pieces and stuff them tightly with a stick!
LEVEL 4:
Brick Master
Weigh, seal, and store your ecobrick—you're ready to build!
